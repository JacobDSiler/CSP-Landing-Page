Cut your mesh.

ZBrush-style interactive curve cutting for Blender. Trim, slice, and carve meshes with precision — in Object, Sculpt, and Edit mode.

Features
Everything you need to cut
✂

Three Cut Modes

Trim removes one side. Slice splits into two pieces. Intersect extracts shapes. All with a single drawn curve.

⭕

Closed Loop Cuts

Draw a closed shape to punch holes or extract regions. Inward and outward modes detected automatically.

🎨

Live Visualization

See exactly what will be removed before you cut. Red overlay shows the affected area in real time with ray-clipped precision.

🗿

Sculpt Mode

Cut directly while sculpting. The addon handles mode transitions automatically — cut and keep sculpting.

◼

Auto Remesh

Voxel, QuadriFlow, or QuadRemesher — clean topology after every cut. Configurable and automatic.

🔒

View Lock

The viewport locks while drawing so you never accidentally rotate your view mid-cut. Focus on precision.

Workflow
Works where you work
📦

Object Mode

The standard workflow. Select your target, draw, cut.

🗿

Sculpt Mode

Cut without leaving sculpt. Returns you to sculpting after the cut.

✏️

Edit Mode

Precise cuts during modeling. Automatic mode transitions.
